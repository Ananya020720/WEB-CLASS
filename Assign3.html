Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassic artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres and today hangs in the lovere. It is the hird of three portraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year



Date: 1806
Medium: Old canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Marie Du Lovere, Paris
